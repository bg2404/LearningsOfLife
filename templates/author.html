About the Author

Priya Goel is a qualified Chartered Accountant, working in a private bank as an internal auditor. She has interest in writing and is pursuing her passion by writing blogs. She was awarded the 'Best Orator' from her alma matar. She was awarded the 'Student of the Year' in 2013.

The author hopes to share her experiences through blogs and stories. The same may give the readers a different perspective to look at different aspects of the life and inspire them.
